Config Management Camp

3 and 4 February 2014

Gent, Belgium

Full Continuous Delivery with Chef

You are already using Chef, but you want to implement Continuous Delivery and you're not sure where to start? Well then this talk is for you! We'll cover general workflow considerations when starting to tackle the problem of implementing Continuous Delivery. We'll use a Git and Jenkins specific development workflow as a use case for understanding how to turn that high-level view of considerations into a working pipeline. We'll look at Chef specific constructs that are especially helpful and give you a couple of helpful tools when building your own pipelines using Chef.

George's slides are available on slideshare.

About George Miranda

George (@gmiranda23) is a Senior Consultant with Chef. He helps new Chefs level up their automation skills and enjoys helping folks tackle difficult infrastructure problems. He spent many years in the video game industry and has worked in organizations of various size from startups to large scale Enterprises. For over 15 years, he's enjoyed solving cultural issues just as much as technical issues especially since they go together like peas and carrots.

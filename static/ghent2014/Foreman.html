#Cfgmgmtcamp

Config Management Camp

3 and 4 February 2014

Gent, Belgium

Foreman Community

Foreman is a complete lifecycle management tool for physical and virtual servers. Through deep integration with configuration management, DHCP, DNS, TFTP, and PXE and Image-based unattended installations, Foreman manages every stage of the lifecycle of your physical or virtual servers. Foreman provides comprehensive, auditable interaction facilities including a web frontend and robust, RESTful API.

Join us in the Foreman Community Room as we provide some introductory, intermediate-to-advanced and time for open spaces and hacking.

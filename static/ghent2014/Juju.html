#Cfgmgmtcamp

Config Management Camp

3 and 4 February 2014

Gent, Belgium

Juju Track

Juju is a service orchestration tool for the cloud. Configure, manage, maintain, deploy and scale efficiently with best-practice Charms on any public, private or hybrid cloud from a powerful GUI or the command-line.

Users are encouraged to bring their laptops and configure Juju if they want to follow along. We will also cover installation during the introduction.

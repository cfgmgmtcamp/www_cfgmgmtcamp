#Cfgmgmtcamp

2 and 3 February 2015

Gent, Belgium

Puppet Community Room

Join us in the Puppet Community Room on February 2 and 3 at Config Management Camp for presentations, and stay on February 4 for our third Puppet Contributor Summit for more Puppet fun, including lightning talks and plenty of time for hacking on Puppet projects.

If you want to stay an extra day for the Puppet Contributor Summit on February 4, please register to help us plan!

Agenda

Monday, February 2

  • 14:00 - Future of Puppet - Luke Kanies
  • 14:40 - Managing applications with Puppet - David Lutterkort (slides)
  • 15:40 - Modern Module Development - Morgan Haskell (slides)
  • 16:20 - Testing with Beaker - Liam Bennett (slides)

Tuesday, February 3

  • 11:20 - Puppet Performance Profiling - R.I.Pienaar (slides)
  • 12:00 - When You Can't Move Fast and Break Things - Moses Mendoza (slides)
  • 14:00 - Puppet Master to Puppet Server - Kevin Corcoran (slides)
  • 14:40 - Deployment orchestration - Jan Collijs (slides)
  • 15:00 - Advantages and Challenges of Deploying OpenStack with Puppet - Colleen Murphy (slides)

Other talks you might be interested in seeing in the main track

  • Monday, February 2 at 12:00 - Panel: Next Steps in Configuration Management with Luke Kanies
  • Tuesday, February 3 at 12:00 - Panel: Provisioning Cloud Infrastructure as Code with Gareth Rushgrove and Mitchell Hashimoto

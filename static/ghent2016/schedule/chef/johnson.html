#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Infrastructure testing

Kimball Johnson - Monday, February 1, 16:20-17:00 - B3.019

In this talk I will discuss Infrastructure testing using Test Kitchen and other tools, covering both the methods to perform infrastructure testing and the reasons behind it Although test kitchen is a tool maintained by Chef, I will not be limiting my talk to just Chef specifics.

About Kimball Johnson

Kimball Johnson has been programming since a very early age, starting with BBC Micros, then MS DOS and Windows Systems, however was enlightened with a copy of Debian GNU/Linux Woody at university. From this developed an affinity to Systems Administration, but like all good admins he is lazy and so tries to automate as much of his job as possible, and now follows the way of the Infrastructure as Code, most recently working for Chef Software Inc.

#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Hosts' lifecycle with Foreman and Ansible

Daniel Lobato Garcia

Ansible - Tuesday, 2/2/2016 14:40 - Ansible

"Ansible is a extremely easy to learn tool to orchestrate and configure hosts. However, managing the whole lifecycle of your hosts will require other tools for provisioning and monitoring at least. At the Foreman project we have found Ansible to be a fundamental tool on a lot of deployments for configuration, so we have started efforts to provide value to Ansible users. This talk will show you what can you gain out of using Foreman with Ansible, including providing an UI for remote execution, hosts registration, provisioning, and more. By the end of it, you should at least be intrigued to consider using Foreman to complement your automat ion efforts with Ansible."

About Daniel Lobato Garcia

Daniel Lobato Garcia works as a software engineer at Red Hat and started the foreman_ansible plugin to allow Ansible hosts to register in Foreman and other integrations. He cares about making Foreman provide the maximum value for users and still being easy to deploy and use.

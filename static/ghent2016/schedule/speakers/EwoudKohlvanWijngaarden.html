#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Foreman and PowerDNS

Ewoud Kohl van Wijngaarden

Foreman - Tuesday , 2/2/2016 15:40-16:00 B2.015

Foreman 1.10 introduces support for DNS modules. I took the opportunity to make use of my favorite DNS server and resolve an issue that was opened 2.5 years earlier. In this talk I will talk about my experiences writing the plugin and give a short demo.

About Ewoud Kohl van Wijngaarden

Ewoud used the Foreman and Puppet at his previous job where he was part dev, part ops. Currently he's a full time (python) dev, but his ops colleagues do use Foreman and Puppet. He's still part of the Foreman Installer team where he mostly reviews puppet patches.

#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Building a private cloud with the OpenStack charms

James Page

Juju - Monday ,1/2/2016 16:20 - B4.039

OpenStack is one of the most complex pieces of software an admin can deploy today. This instruction session will include not just deploying a cloud, but lifecycle management. We'll cover upgrades, data backup, and networking considerations.

About James Page

James as been involved in Open Source software since 2000, evangelising and delivering the use of Free and Open Source technologies in a major UK bank. In 2010, James discovered Ubuntu and became involved in both the development of Ubuntu and shortly afterwards OpenStack. James is part of the team responsible for delivering and supporting OpenStack as part of every Ubuntu release and for the Juju Charms for OpenStack, the best way for deploying and managing OpenStack deployments on Ubuntu at any scale.

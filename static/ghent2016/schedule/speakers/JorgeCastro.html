Introduction to Juju

Jorge Castro

Juju - Monday, 1/1/2016 14:00-14:40 B4.039

This introductory talk will include installation of Juju and configuration on multiple clouds. We'll also cover Juju terminology and basics, as well as a primer on how to leverage Juju to integrate with other configuration management tools.

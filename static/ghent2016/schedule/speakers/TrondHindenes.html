#cfgmgmtcamp

1 and 2 February 2016

Gent, Belgium

Ansible + Windows

Trond Hindenes

Ansible - Monday, February 1 - 15:40 - Ansible room

"Ansible's newfound love for Windows opens up a new world of opportunities on the Windows platform. This session will cover the basics of what Ansible is and how it works on Windows, then dive into developing PowerShell-based modules for Ansible. The session will also cover how Ansible and DSC fits together, and lastly a few words on how to provision Azure resources from Ansible."

About Trond Hindenes

Trond Hindenes is the CTO/Chief Architect at Pundit Consulting in Norway. Trond as done a mix of consulting and internal operations/engineering and mainly focuses on automation projects on-prem or in Azure. Trond is also a contributor on several of the PowerShell modules included in Ansible.

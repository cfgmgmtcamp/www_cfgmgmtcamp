#cfgmgmtcamp

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

Containers, runtime environments and configuration

Christhope Furmaniak, Monday , February 6, 15:40 - 16:20 B.CON

Docker (and containers in general) are strongly bound to the promise that one image can fit all environments and that you can run the same bits on all your runtime environments (from dev/test to production) without rebuilding images. But how can you achieve that goal in a practical and smart way while being able to keep track of the history of the configuration of your applications? This talk will exhibit how git and confd can help you succeed in this critical mission without loosing the flexibility required so that both your dev and your ops can live together in perfect harmony. A demo will be made using Rancher as the docker orchestrator

About Speaker

Christophe is a passionate developer who worked for 15 years in an Ops organization. He is now consultant for Zenika and its points of interests are dealing with the DevOps culture, Software Architecture and Application Lifecycle management.

Drop OS images on bare-metal

Luk치코 Zapletal - Tuesday, Feb 7, 15:40 - 16:00 - B2.015

First, it starts with a DVD with an OS installer, then is a recipe to automate it. And then PXE booting from network which sometimes lead to discovering new bare-metal nodes. Some shops fully automate provisioning and the only action is to power on a server. We all have that. But what's next?

Make it fast. Blazing fast. Do it virtualization-like or cloud-like experience with Foreman Discovery and the new image-deployment capabilities.

6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

Resilient Systems Require Resilient People

Hannah Foxwell - Monday, February 6, 11:20 - 12:00 - D.AUD

Resilience: The ability to recover quickly from difficulties

Building resilient systems is what we do and we do it well, but how much time do we spend working on our own personal resilience? In the ever changing world of technology, how do we ensure we are flexible, adaptable and resilient in the face of challenges and setbacks?

In this talk we’ll look at ways in which we can improve the resilience of our organisations, our teams and ourselves. Because if your team isn’t ready for change, your platform isn’t either.

Slides are available on Speaker Deck.

About Hannah Foxwell

Hannah is Product Manager at Server Density - HumanOps champion, HugOps evangelist and DevOps believer. Having spent most of her career trying to create great working environments for Developers, her focus now shifts towards Ops.

Hannah believes that the health of your infrastructure is not just about hardware, software, automation and uptime - It also includes the health and wellbeing of your team.

Outside of work you can generally find Hannah in her local pub enjoying a craft beer while planning her next adventure.

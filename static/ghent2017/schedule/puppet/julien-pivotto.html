6 and 7 February 2017

Gent, Belgium

Puppet DSL: Back to the basics

Julien Pivotto - Monday, Feb 6, 17:20 - 17:40 - B1.015

We use Puppet everyday but sometimes we are so deeply involved in our daily jobs that we forget to take a look at upstream documentation.

This short talk will enlight some less-used Puppet DSL tricks and tips that anyone can use to improve its Puppet code. It is targeted at both new and old users; anyone should pick some cool tips here.

Puppet DSL: back to the basics from Julien Pivotto

About Speaker

Julien Pivotto is a young Open-Source consultant at Inuits where he is helping organisations with the deployment of long-term solutions based on Open-Source infrastructure.

He is a strong believer in the devops movement and has technical focus towards infrastructure automation, continuous integration, monitoring and high availability.

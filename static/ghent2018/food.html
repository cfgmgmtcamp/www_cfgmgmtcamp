Food

Lunch

Lunch is available in the campus cafeteria/canteen at your own expense in the 'Resto D' opposite the D auditorium.
If you are a speaker, organizer or sponsor, please find an organizer for your food voucher.

The main menu items for each day are listed here, there is also a soup of the day and a dessert.
There is a western vegetarian, an eastern vegetarian, a vegan, and a gluten-free option, please inquire with the cafeteria staff.

Monday 5 February 2018

  • Gyros
  • Vol-au-vent
  • Spaghetti
  • Baked omelette

Tuesday 6 February 2018

  • "Zwitserse Schijf"
  • Vol-au-vent
  • Spaghetti
  • Quorn with coconut curry

Wednesday 7 February 2018

  • Meatballs
  • Vol-au-vent
  • Spaghetti
  • Vegetarian quiche
A meal costs 7,70 EUR, there is an assortment of desserts ranging from 1,40 EUR to 2,50 EUR.
Please note that all prices advertised in the cafeteria are the only correct prices.
The cafeteria accepts vouchers, cash money and bank cards, such as Bancontact/Mister Cash, Maestro, MasterCard and VISA.

Beverages

There will be beverages, such as coffee, tea, and water served in the foyer.
